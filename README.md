# Policy.AI
Often times Americans are left in the dark when it comes to understanding and engaging in the legislative process for the US Government. To teach and engage more Americans in a civic capacity for their country I will be leveraging AI to analyze proposed and passed legislation on the federal level and help users navigate and understand these documents as it relates to them. I will be pulling from public data made available by the US Congress as the main source. This web application will be entirely free and will not require any user information to be inputted into the application.
